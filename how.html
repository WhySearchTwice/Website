Getting Started

The Retrospect Chrome extension collects your browsing history across all of your devices and provides you with an always up-to-date window into your browsing history. Getting started is simple:

1. Install the Chrome extension

We will ask for you email address so we can tie your multiple devices back to you.

2. Sync the extension to other devices

In the background Chrome will install the extension on all of your other devices. The next time you use your other devices you may be asked to provide your email address again.

3. Browse the web!

The pages you visit will automatically be loaded into the extension, allowing you to view them in the timeline immediately from any device.

Viewing your Timeline:

After installing Retrospect your default history page will be replaced with the Retrospect timeline. Click the history icon to view a real-time representation of your web browsing. From here you can view any pages you have visited on any devices, search for pages in your history, and view interesting statistics about your web browsing habits.
