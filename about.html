Our Story

Retrospect began as a project for our capstone in the University of Washington Information School. We started discussing the idea in Summer 2012 because each of us wants a better way to utilize our web history. As heavy content consumers, the web is very much a substantial part of our lives. Retrospect is a project to better understand how we browse and improve the web browsing experience.
